Privacy Policy

Air Type respects your privacy and is committed to protecting user data.

Information We Collect

Air Type uses Google Sign-In through Google Cloud Services to allow users to sign in. When you sign in using Google, we collect your email address for authentication purposes only.

How We Use Information

  • Email address is used only to authenticate users.
  • We do not use your email for marketing or advertising.
  • We do not sell or rent user data.

Data Sharing

Air Type does not share personal data with any third parties. Authentication is securely handled by Google Cloud as a service provider.

Data Storage and Security

  • All authentication data is managed securely by Google Cloud.
  • Data is encrypted during transmission.
  • Air Type does not store personal data on its own servers.

Network Usage

Air Type connects your smartphone and PC over a local Wi-Fi network. No internet connection is required for typing functionality.

User Rights

Users may request deletion of their account and associated data at any time by contacting support.

Children’s Privacy

Air Type is not designed specifically for children under the age of 13 and does not knowingly collect personal data from children.
